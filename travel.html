Places to Travel!

Jeju Island - Korea

While Jeju Island may be known more for it's emerald waters and sandy beaches, it also has the tallest mountain in Korea at 1,950 meters (6,398 feet). Hallasan Mountain rises up in the center of Jeju Island and has over 350 volcano shaped peaks. - luxeadventuretraveler.com

Things to do:

  • Hiking
  • Eating the seafood & all the food the Korean cuisine has to offer!!
  • Visiting their unlimited amounts of museums!

Hawaii - USA

Hawaii is known for its beautiful beaches—some of them with unusual colors. Many beaches are filled with white sand, but other Hawaiian shores are covered with green, red, pink, and even black sand. Whether you like hiking, biking, kayaking, sailing, swimming, or just sitting on the beach, Hawaii is the state for you. - nationalgeographic.com

Things to do:

  • Hiking
  • Eating the fruits & all the food the Hawaiian cuisine has to offer!!
  • Visiting their shopping district!

Cannes - France

Cannes, a resort town on the French Riviera, is famed for its international film festival. Its Boulevard de la Croisette, curving along the coast, is lined with sandy beaches, upmarket boutiques and palatial hotels. It’s also home to the Palais des Festivals et des Congrès, a modern building complete with red carpet and Allée des Étoiles – Cannes’ walk of fame. ― Google

Things to do:

  • Sailing
  • Eating the food & all the food the French cuisine has to offer!!
  • Shopping... and/or The Palais des Festivals of Cannes
